About Me

My name is Alkis I. Nerakis, a dedicated physiotherapist specializing in Neurological and Pediatric Rehabilitation. I hold a BSc in Physiotherapy with Honours from Manchester Metropolitan University (MMU) and an MSc in Pediatric Neurological Rehabilitation with Distinction from Oxford Brookes University (OBU).

Throughout my career, I have been committed to continuous professional development (CPD), attending numerous seminars and conferences to enhance my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in physiotherapy.

Additionally, I have gained valuable experience through various placements and positions, including at ΘΗΣΕΑΣ Rehabilitation Center, ΦΙΛΟΚΤΗΤΗΣ Rehabilitation Center, and other professional roles in the healthcare sector.

My Services

  • Neurological Rehabilitation: Treatment for neurological conditions like stroke, multiple sclerosis, and cerebral palsy.
  • Pediatric Physiotherapy: Specialized care for children with various developmental and neurological conditions.
  • Orthopaedic Therapy: Rehabilitation for bone and joint issues, including post-fracture care.
  • Cardiorespiratory Rehabilitation: Programs to aid recovery from cardiac and respiratory conditions.
  • Injury Prevention: Programs designed to prevent injury and improve physical health.
  • Ergonomics: Workplace posture assessments and advice for improving health in the work environment.
